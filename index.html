Tap here to install the app

Important: Trust the Developer Certificate

After installing the app, follow these steps to trust the developer certificate:

  1. Open the Settings app on your device.
  2. Go to General > Device Management or Profiles & Device Management.
  3. Under the Enterprise App section, select the profile for your app's developer.
  4. Tap Trust [Developer Name].
  5. Confirm by tapping Trust again in the pop-up dialog.

Once you've trusted the certificate, you can open and use the app normally.
